First read the information about attire for interviews then answer the question that follows.

For Women

  • Skirts should not be above the knee
  • No open-toe shoes
  • Shirts should fit properly
  • Heels should be 1”-2”
  • Minimum perfume
  • Neutral colored clothes(navy,black ect.)
  • No visible tattoos
  • No visible accessories (body piercing, necklace ect.)
  • No extreme colors

For Men

  • Avoid extreme colors
  • Have a well-fitted blazer
  • Dark shoes and socks
